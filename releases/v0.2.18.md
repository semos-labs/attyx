# Attyx v0.2.18

## Fixes

- **Session restoration** — reconnecting to the daemon after a disconnect or hot-upgrade now properly restores your tabs and panes. Previously, a daemon restart could leave you with a blank session; now the UI rebuilds your layout from the daemon's state, preserving your working context.

## Improvements

- **Smaller release binary** — production builds are now stripped, reducing the final binary size.
